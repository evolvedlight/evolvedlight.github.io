title: AirBnb and getting a B Permit
---

Many guides are recommending AirBnb as a good first accomodation while looking for a long term rent, and while sorting out permits and banks. It is, but there are issues to be aware of. The main drawback is that the standard paperwork you get with an AirBnb is not sufficient for getting a B Permit, as it is classed as a hotel. You need to make sure that your AirBnb landlord can give you a proper signed stamped accomodation contract, as otherwise getting a B permit will not be possible!
